Counselling

Psychotherapeutic counselling is a type of therapy used to treat emotional problems and mental health conditions.

Counselling involves talking to a qualified therapist, allowing you to look deeper into your problems and worries and dealing with troublesome habits and behaviours. This can help you to explore the root of your issues and help to find solutions and strategies so that you can move forward.

Hypnotherapy

Hypnotherapy is a safe and natural therapy which allows you to become more self-aware and more able to take steps towards a better lifestyle.

During hypnosis, clients enter a relaxed trance-like state but remain in control at all times. In this state, the conscious mind is suppressed and the sub-conscious is revealed. The latter is where our instincts, memories and habit-type behaviours are stored and under hypnosis, the therapist can make appropriate, often life-changing suggestions.
