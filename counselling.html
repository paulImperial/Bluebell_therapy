I offer integrative counselling. This means that I can draw on different theoretical approaches and models to find one that really suits you and your personal needs. I believe that this flexibility is really important when treating different individuals.

How does counselling help?

It is often difficult to 'see the wood for the trees' when we experience challenges in our lives. Talking issues through with a trained counsellor often helps the client to begin to see things more clearly and empowers them to start to take control of their situation. During each counselling session, clients can talk freely and without judgment in a way that is often difficult with family or friends. Everything is confidential and there is a respect and acceptance between client and counsellor. Clients are not directed towards particular outcomes; instead they are helped to be able to make the right decisions for themselves. This may involve talking through various options in a supportive environment.

Counselling help you to:

  • explore your thoughts and feelings.
  • have a greater awareness about aspects of your life which may be challenging
  • develop more effective coping strategies
  • manage your emotions and relationships

Counselling can help with various issues, including:

  • low mood or depression
  • anxiety
  • stress caused by work or study
  • lack of confidence
  • relationship difficulties
  • low self esteeem
  • bereavement and loss
  • anger management
  • coping with illness
  • caring for others
  • loneliness

Counselling helps you to create positive change in your life.
